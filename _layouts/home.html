Our Vision

Dominate the Classroom

We strive each and every season to be crowned Academic State Champions.

Win Multiple Championships

We strive to win multiple championships each season (league, districts, regionals, state).

Be a Program of Class

We will compete harder and play tougher than anyone, but always respect the game and our opponents. We beat the Game.

Make Lifelong Impacts

We strive to make lifelong impacts, so players will leave our program more prepared to succeed in life.

Maximize Player Potential

We don't know what the ceiling is for each of our players. Our goal is to work relentlessly to develop our players and raise the ceiling as high as it can go.
